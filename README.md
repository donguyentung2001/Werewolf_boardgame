A browser werewolf boardgame, made with Django, HTML, CSS, Javascript. 

A demonstration of game mechanics: 

Upon entering the website, the user is asked to log in or register for an account if they don't have one. 

<img width="1440" alt="Screen Shot 2020-08-24 at 4 38 59 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/54921286/91106822-8d2f7780-e628-11ea-8a39-0d2275cfbc83.png">

You can enter the room name that you want to enter:
<img width="1440" alt="Screen Shot 2020-08-24 at 4 39 13 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/54921286/91106958-f44d2c00-e628-11ea-8b07-a81d07729e6d.png"> 

You will wait for other users to join. You can talk to others whenever you see the chat box. 
<img width="936" alt="Screen Shot 2020-08-24 at 4 27 59 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/54921286/91107060-31192300-e629-11ea-8161-4625e3e91bdf.png">

In this game, you are the werewolf. You click "werewolf turn" to vote for who you want to kill in this turn. 

<img width="806" alt="Screen Shot 2020-08-25 at 11 44 56 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/54921286/91215089-ddf6ad00-e6c8-11ea-8203-b6744ffa85d2.png">

Once you finish voting, you'll see that the person you wishes to kill is displayed as "Dead". 
<img width="955" alt="Screen Shot 2020-08-25 at 11 45 26 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/54921286/91215093-df27da00-e6c8-11ea-8c9b-30aa6a77902d.png">

After discussing with other villagers in the chat, you can click to vote once you have made your mind on who to execute. 
<img width="1126" alt="Screen Shot 2020-08-25 at 11 45 54 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/54921286/91215098-e0f19d80-e6c8-11ea-89c1-a338a9cbae9b.png">

You have won the game as the person killed in the last turn is a villager, making the number of werewolves equal to that of villagers.
